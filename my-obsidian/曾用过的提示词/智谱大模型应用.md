### *文本转语音*

```bash
curl --request POST \
  --url https://open.bigmodel.cn/api/paas/v4/audio/speech \
  --header 'Authorization: Bearer 5e58910a65cd43869e73adc42869772d.V2T1Gn7ccArKGiM8' \
  --header 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  --data '{
  "model": "cogtts",
  "input": "任务已完成，请问还有什么可以帮你的",
  "voice": "tongtong",
  "response_format": "wav",
  "watermark_enabled": false,
  "volume": 1,
  "speed": 1
}' --output a.wav
```
